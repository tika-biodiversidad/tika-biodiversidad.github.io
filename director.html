Field Exploration

Field research across high-Andean puna, cloud forests, dry forests, coastal lomas and glacial environments. My work combines plant collection, ecological observation, biodiversity monitoring and scientific storytelling.

Research & Taxonomy

Specialist in Neotropical plant systematics with particular interest in Asteraceae, Solanaceae, Brassicaceae, Malvaceae, Violaceae and Amaryllidaceae. Research integrates morphology, distribution modelling, herbarium studies and conservation assessments.
