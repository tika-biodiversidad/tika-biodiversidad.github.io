Field Exploration

Field research across high-Andean puna, glacial landscapes, cloud forests, dry forests and coastal lomas. My work combines specimen collection, ecological observation, biodiversity monitoring and scientific storytelling.

Research & Taxonomy

Specialist in Neotropical plant systematics with particular interest in Asteraceae, Solanaceae, Brassicaceae, Malvaceae, Violaceae and Amaryllidaceae. Research integrates morphology, herbarium studies, distribution modelling and conservation assessments.
