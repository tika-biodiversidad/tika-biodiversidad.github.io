About TIKA

Who we are

TIKA is a scientific initiative dedicated to the study, conservation, and appreciation of Andean plant diversity. We believe science must be accessible to everyone and that knowledge is a fundamental tool for biodiversity conservation.

Through research, education, outreach, and technical services, we connect science with society.

Mission

To promote research, education, and conservation of Andean plants and ecosystems.

Vision

A future where Andean biodiversity is known, valued, and conserved by society.

Values

🌱 Scientific rigor
🔍 Transparency
🧭 Integrity
🤝 Inclusion
🌍 Collaboration
🌿 Conservation
🎓 Education
🏔️ Andes commitment

Team

TIKA is built by researchers, students, and collaborators working together for Andean biodiversity conservation. Our work integrates field exploration, scientific research, and education to generate real impact.
