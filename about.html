About TIKA

Transforming Research into Conservation Action for Andean Biodiversity

Plants sustain life and understanding them is the first step toward conservation

Who we are

TIKA is a scientific initiative dedicated to the study, conservation, and appreciation of Andean plant diversity. We believe science must be accessible to everyone and that knowledge is a fundamental tool for biodiversity conservation.

Through scientific research, education, outreach, and technical services, we bring botanical knowledge closer to students, professionals, communities, and institutions.

Mission

To promote research, education, and conservation of Andean plants and ecosystems.

Vision

A future where Andean biodiversity is known, valued, and conserved by society.
