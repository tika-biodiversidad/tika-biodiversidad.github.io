About TIKA

TIKA is a scientific initiative focused on the study, conservation, and education of Andean plant diversity.

Mission & Vision

Mission: Promote research, education, and conservation of Andean ecosystems.

Vision: A world where Andean biodiversity is fully understood and protected.
