About TIKA

TIKA is a scientific initiative focused on the study, conservation, and education of Andean plant diversity. We connect research, fieldwork, and education to support biodiversity conservation.

Mission & Vision

Mission: Promote scientific research, education, and conservation of Andean ecosystems.

Vision: A world where Andean biodiversity is fully understood, valued, and protected.
