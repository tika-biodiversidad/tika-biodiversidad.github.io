About TIKA

TIKA is a scientific initiative dedicated to the study, conservation, and education of Andean biodiversity. We aim to bridge science and society through accessible knowledge and field-based research.

Mission & Vision

Mission: Promote research, education, and conservation of Andean ecosystems.

Vision: A world where Andean biodiversity is fully understood, valued, and protected.
