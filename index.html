About TIKA

TIKA is a scientific initiative dedicated to the study, conservation, and dissemination of Andean biodiversity. We integrate field research, taxonomy, education, and conservation action.

Versión en español disponible próximamente.

Mission & Vision

Mission: Promote scientific research, education, and conservation of Andean ecosystems.

Vision: A world where Andean biodiversity is fully understood and effectively protected.

Scientific Approach

Taxonomy

Identification and classification of plant diversity.

Field Ecology

Exploration and documentation of Andean ecosystems.

Conservation

Evidence-based strategies for biodiversity protection.

Programs

  • Academy TIKA — scientific training
  • Field Explorations — biodiversity surveys
  • Conservation Program — restoration actions
  • Open Science Library — educational resources

Impact

Research
